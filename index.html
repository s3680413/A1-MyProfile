Project Idea

The project is about a mobile flight app. This app is mainly created to be used by adults. It is about using mobile app phones for booking flights, getting your seat number, gate number and so on. This project will allow the users to use it for free. In addition, the app also helps the users (passengers) to search for cheap flights and to get boarding pass at their own convenience. Furthermore, the project will helps to connect the users to different hotels in the world.

This project is useful to customers in many ways. First of all, it provides convenience to customers. Many airlines require passengers to be at the airport four hours before boarding time to check in their luggage, get their boarding pass and seat number, etc. This app helps customers to take their time to get boarding pass, seat number at their own convenience. And especially to customers who always in a rush but do not have any baggage to check in. In addition to that, it disseminates information faster. For example, there will be changes to departure time, flight gates and sometimes it quite hard to hear announcement at the airport. So, having this app will help you get notifications and updates about these changes

First of all, one feature of this app is ‘Flight Tracker’. This feature is for tracking flights. This includes, how far you are from your departure place, destination, also display the arrival time, nearest destination and so forth. During the flight, this feature allows passengers to track how long they have been travelling, how far is from their destination and the total distance of their flight. Apart from that, this feature also allows passengers to track multiple flights. For example, if you are on a different flight with your friend, you can check what time she/he will arrive at the airport. Another feature is ‘Flight Video & Songs. During the take-off, passengers will be able to watch movies and listen to songs on this app. This helps passengers not feel boring during their flight. In addition to that, ‘App guidance’ is another one. Guidelines and tips are helpfully shown in this app to guide people. This is the most important feature in the app which will help the user to get familiar with using the app. It provide information of how to track the flight, using some other features like playing music and movie. This helps in reducing the education of passengers or users of how to use the app. Furthermore, Flight Details This will include all details about your flight. Which includes the check-in time, gate number, terminal number, boarding time, flight number, weight of luggage accepted on the plane, seat number and so on.
